Upcoming Events

15 May

Annual Sports Day

Join us for a day of athletic competitions and team spirit. Students will participate in various track and field events, showcasing their physical abilities and sportsmanship. Parents are encouraged to attend and cheer for their children.

22 May

Parent-Teacher Conference

Discuss your child's progress and academic goals with our teachers. This is an excellent opportunity to gain insights into your child's strengths and areas for improvement. Please schedule your appointment in advance to ensure availability.

05 Jun

Science Fair

Explore innovative projects and experiments by our talented students. The fair will showcase a wide range of scientific disciplines, from biology to physics. This event is open to all parents and community members interested in supporting young scientists.

12 Jun

Visitation Day

Parents are invited to visit the school and spend time with their children. This is a great opportunity to observe classroom activities, interact with teachers, and see your child's learning environment firsthand. Light refreshments will be provided.

19 Jun

Music, Dance & Drama

Experience the artistic talents of our students in this spectacular showcase. The event will feature musical performances, dance routines, and dramatic presentations from students across all grade levels. Come support our young artists and enjoy an evening of entertainment.
