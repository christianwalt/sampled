School Uniform

At Pearlworth Foundation School, we believe that a smart and consistent uniform helps create a sense of unity and pride among our students. Our uniform policy is designed to ensure all students are dressed appropriately for their academic environment.

Boy's School Uniform:

  • White Shirt: Short-sleeved, button-up
  • Green Sweater/Vest: Sleeveless sweater or vest with a school logo on the chest
  • Grey Shorts: Knee-length grey shorts
  • Green Socks: Knee-high green socks
  • Black Shoes: Standard school shoes

Girl's School Uniform:

  • White Shirt: Short-sleeved, button-up
  • Green Sweater/Vest: Sleeveless sweater or vest with a school logo on the chest
  • Red Tartan Skirt: Knee-length pleated skirt in a red and green tartan pattern
  • Red Socks: Knee-high red socks
  • Black Shoes: Standard school shoes

Please ensure all items are clearly labeled with the student's name. Uniforms can be purchased from our designated school uniform supplier.
